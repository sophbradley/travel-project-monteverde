Why Monteverde?

Monteverde - The Cloud Forest

Visiting Monteverde is a trip like no other! The cloud forest is situated 1,440m (4,662ft) above sea level. Due to this elevation Monteverde's forests are blessed with high amount of precipitation resulting in incredibly lush jungles, and a unique mix of flora and fauna. The cloud forest boasts a wide variety of plant and animal life, including sloths and hundreds of species of orchids endemic to the area.

Along with hiking to experience the amazing nature that surrounds you, visitors seeking more adrenaline can also zip-line through the canopy or take a horse trek across the mountains. After a day of adventure there's plenty of wonderful restaurants and a wide variety of accommdation to enjoy in the lovely town of Santa Elena - the closest town to the cloud forest.

RESTAURANTS

Recommendations for places to eat

Taco Taco Taqueria

This is a great place to go for delicious Mexican food - tacos (of course), tamales, elotes, burritos, bowls and salads. They also have a great selection of plant-based food for non-meat eaters. Plus great beers and cocktails!

Address:

Puntarenas Province, Santa Elena, 60109

What I like about it

The staff are friendly, the food is super tasty, and the portions are big - what more could you ask for?!

Don Luis

Don Luis is a lovely restaurant for dinner, options include meat and seafood dishes, pizzas and some vegetarian option. Prices for the meat and seafood can be a little more than elsewhere in Santa Elena, but the food is worth it!

Address:

Monteverde Puntarenas, 60109, Costa Rica

What I like about it

The service is impecable and the food is amazing, well worth going for a fantastic meal out!

Open Kitchen

This restaurant / coffee shop in the heart of Santa Elena is a fantastic place to go for breakfast, lunch, or dinner! The menu is comprised of mediterranean and local dishes, and are packed with flavour!

Address:

Puntarenas Province, Monteverde 60101

What I like about it

There's a varied menu with vegan and vegetarian options available, the food is delicious, and as it is in the middle of town it's really easy to get to.
